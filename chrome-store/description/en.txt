TRS on Steroids improves day-to-day ticket handling inside the The Config Team support portal.

It is designed to reduce repetitive work while keeping ticket updates more consistent and visible during normal support workflows.

Current features include:
- extracting live ticket context from the portal
- quick comment template application from a dropdown
- inline ticket warnings for missing or risky conditions
- visibility of time usage against quoted effort
- optional browser-AI helpers for summaries and key points when supported by the browser

The extension currently focuses more on workflow improvements than on AI. AI features are optional helpers, not the core product.

Examples of inline warnings include:
- missing next contact date
- missing delivery date for Change Requests
- overdue next contact or delivery dates
- unassigned tickets
- stale customer-facing communication
- low or exceeded quoted time

Privacy:
- Personal information is not collected or sold by the extension.
- No external service is required for the core workflow.
